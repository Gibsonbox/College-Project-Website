What software do I use and recommend?

I would always recommend getting to know the software you are using before moving into 3rd party software. Music software can be very expensive and a lot of DAWs provide some excellent plugins that are underrated. Always make as much use of what you have initially paid before before moving on to other plugins, even if they are what's considered industry standard. Vital is a wavetable synthesiser which works by using an audio file, or wavetable, as an oscillator. What makes this form of synthesis unique is that the wavetable can be cycled through from the start to the end which creates a unique modulation effect. Like other synthesisers, you can combine oscillators to make complex and unique sounds which are difficult to recreate. What makes Vital such a great synthesiser is that it encourages the user to experiment around with modulation effects. Built into the main graphical user interface are several windows that hold parameters to control low frequency oscillators (LFOs). LFOs are useful as they slowly modulate intensity from low to high and this can be applied to several other parameters within the synthesiser. Vital is great as it promotes this form of experimentation so the user can have fun with the software.

Compressors are a dynamic process which allows you to shape and control a sound which is played into it. Compressors can be used clinically, in that they can cut or add to a sound for very specific reasons. Or compressors can be used creatively whereby you can use aggressive or heavy processing to achieve unconventional or unique sounds from what you put through them. Compressors are often described as something which can never be truly mastered. Each compressor handles sound differently and this is what is described as character. OTT is a very common compressor used in electronic dance music but is also making appearances in modern trap and hip hop music. The compressor is known to handle huge amounts of sound processing and adds its own flair to the sound which is almost always noticeable if you know the compressor well. Many people even play sound through the compressor just to add its characater with no further processing being carried out.
